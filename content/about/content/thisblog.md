- **网站引擎**：[Hugo](https://gohugo.io/)  
- **网站主题**：[Hugo-theme-monochrome](https://github.com/kaiiiz/)  
- **评论系统**：[Twikoo](https://twikoo.js.org/)|[Artalk](https://artalk.js.org/)    
- **图片灯箱**：[Glightbox](https://biati-digital.github.io/glightbox/)|[Fancybox](https://fancyapps.com/fancybox/)
- **图片瀑布流**：[Masonry](https://masonry.desandro.com/)
- **音乐模块**：[Aplayer](https://aplayer.js.org/)
- **地图模块**：[Leaflet](https://leafletjs.com/)
- **图片存储**：[S.EE(原SM.MS)](https://s.ee/)
- **评论区数据库**：[MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/)
- **简单数据存储**：[Gist](https://gist.github.com/)
- **滑动渐入动画**：[AOS](http://michalsnik.github.io/aos/)
- **网站托管**：[Github](https://www.github.com) + [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com) 
- **域名购买(可选)**：[Namesilo](https://www.namesilo.com/) 
- **域名托管(可选)**：[Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/)
